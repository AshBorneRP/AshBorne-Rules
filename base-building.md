# 🏰 10. Compounds & Raiding

On AshBorne RolePlay, constructing a base is about establishing your footprint in the world, not hoarding assets. A fortress must generate roleplay and serve a narrative purpose. 

Because of this, raiding is treated as a major server event. It is not a casual loot-run. Breaking into a home should shift the balance of power, spark long-term vendettas, and create incredible stories. Your goal as a raider is to leave a narrative impact, not just to empty crates.

### ⛺ 10.1 Compound Classifications & Limits
A base is defined as any structure that has been fortified (walls, gates, locks) to claim territory. 
* **Primary Base Limit:** Solos and groups are permitted exactly **one** primary fortified base. 
* **Personal Stashes:** Faction members share a collective base, but each member is allowed **one** personal codelock for a private locker or chest within that base. 
* **Outposts:** Factions are allowed one small forward outpost. If the faction expands into a Tier 2 Settlement, the outpost must be dismantled.
* *Note: Using alternate accounts to bypass building limits or create "ghost stashes" will result in a permanent ban.*

#### 🔒 10.1.1 Security Limits (Codelocks)
* **Main Bases & Faction Settlements:** Capped at a maximum of **10 codelocks** (covering all gates, doors, and secured containers).
* **Settler Housing:** Individual civilian homes within a larger approved settlement are granted **3 codelocks** per home.

#### 🏗️ 10.1.2 Construction Requirements
* **RaG Basebuilding** is the mandatory mod for all major construction.
* Vanilla DayZ fences are only permitted as gates in scenarios where RaG assets physically cannot snap into place.

### 🛑 10.2 Roleplay Overrides Game Mechanics
The DayZ engine allows for "exploits" that make zero sense in a realistic environment. "The game let me do it" is not a valid defense.
* **No Internal Dismantling:** You cannot dismantle a wall from the inside to escape a compound you were caught in. 
* **Lock-Shifting:** You can technically hacksaw a lock in-game, but on AshBorne, this is a **Hard Raid** and requires prior staff approval.
* *Rule of thumb: If your action relies on a game mechanic rather than realistic logic, do not do it.*

### 🔓 10.3 Infiltrations (Soft Raiding)
A Soft Raid is a non-destructive break-in. This means bypassing security without breaking locks, destroying walls, or exploiting bugs. **Staff approval is NOT required for a Soft Raid.**

**🟢 Legal Soft Raid Tactics:**
* Walking through an accidentally unlocked gate or missing wall panel.
* Looting from unsecured, accessible storage.
* Leaving "calling cards," writing notes, or stealing flags to initiate a rivalry.

**🔴 Illegal Soft Raid Tactics:**
* **Physics Abuse:** Stacking items (barrels, campfires) or player-boosting (jumping on heads) to vault walls.
* **Glitches:** Clipping through windows or solid walls.
* **Lockjacking:** Placing your own lock on an enemy's gate to lock them out.
* **Dismantling:** Removing *anything* with tools.

### 🧨 10.4 Destructive Breaches (Hard Raiding)
A Hard Raid involves the physical destruction of property (cutting locks, destroying walls). 
**All Hard Raids require explicit approval via a Staff Ticket submitted 48 hours in advance.**

**Your Hard Raid Ticket Must Include:**
1. A significant, lore-accurate reason (revenge, territory, ideology). **Loot is not a reason.**
2. Coordinates and screenshots of the target.
3. Your intended narrative goal once the breach is successful.

#### ⚔️ 10.4.1 Hard Raid Execution
* **Entry:** You may breach exactly **one** exterior entry point. 
* **Precision:** Destroy only what is necessary to navigate. Do not destroy decorative RP items or non-blocking storage.
* **The Breadcrumb:** You must leave a trace (a note or calling card) so the victim has a thread to follow.
* **Proof:** You **must** record the entire raid for potential Staff review.

### 🚫 10.5 Zero Tolerance for Griefing
* No raiding the same base multiple times in a single week.
* No "Loot Cycling" (throwing items on the ground to make them despawn).
* No sabotaging a victim's ability to rebuild out of OOC spite.

### 🔫 10.6 Combat Rules During a Raid
* **Defenders:** If you catch someone actively destroying your walls or cutting locks, you may open fire without verbal warning. 
* **Defenders (Soft Raids):** If you catch someone sneaking in an open door, you **must** verbally initiate before shooting.
* **Attackers:** You may **never** shoot first without proper verbal initiation, regardless of the raid type.
