# ⚙️ 9. Miscellaneous Provisions

Not every system requires its own dedicated chapter, but the following rules are essential for maintaining server stability, immersion, and fair play. These are the final "anchors" that keep the AshBorne experience grounded.

### 🚤 9.1 Strict Vehicle & Vessel Regulations
AshBorne is a world of scarcity. To ensure server performance and prevent "wealth hoarding," we enforce zero-tolerance limits on land vehicles and watercraft. Engines are a luxury, not a right.

* **Solo Survivors:** Limit of **1** functional asset (Land OR Water).
* **Registered Groups & Factions:** Absolute limit of **2** functional assets total (e.g., 1 car and 1 boat, or 2 cars). 
* **Approved Settlements:** May maintain up to **3** communal assets to serve the entire civilian population.

**The Anti-Hoarding Clause:** If staff discovers hidden, "stashed," or "tucked" vehicles intended to bypass these limits, they will be deleted immediately without warning or compensation. Living together makes you a group; you do not get to claim "solo" limits while sharing a base.

### 🎭 9.2 The Overseers (NPC Factions) & Power Balance
In the AshBorne universe, certain "Overseer" factions exist as manifestations of the world's natural order. They are active narrative tools used to ensure no single group becomes a stagnant, unopposed monopoly.

If a group becomes excessively powerful or disruptive without providing quality roleplay, the Overseers may intervene IC:
* **Stage 1:** Ominous warnings, intercepted radio transmissions, or anonymous threats.
* **Stage 2:** Targeted sabotage, IED traps on supply routes, or precision strikes on outposts.
* **Stage 3:** Full-scale ideological warfare.
*You can resist. You can fight back. But know that the world itself will react if you stop contributing to the story.*

### 🛠️ 9.3 Technical Limitations & Compensation
DayZ is an imperfect engine. To manage expectations, the following systems are "Use at Your Own Risk" and are generally ineligible for staff compensation:
* **The "Door" Glitch:** Dropping items near modded single/double doors can result in items falling through the map. **Prevention:** Do not drop loot near doors. No compensation will be provided for lost items here.
* **Vehicle & Boat Physics:** Like land vehicles, boats can fly, capsize, or vanish due to engine desync or "DayZ space program" physics. **Compensation Requirement:** You **must** provide clear video evidence of the glitch to receive a replacement. No video = no help.
* **Unrepairable Assets:** Some rare vehicles or boats in the world cannot be fixed due to a lack of available parts. If an asset becomes completely inoperable, it is a narrative "total loss"—abandon it and move on.
* **Server Instability:** If you continue to play while the server is actively crashing or during an announced maintenance window, any lost gear is your responsibility.

### 📻 9.4 The In-Character (IC) Discord Radio
Our Discord Radio channel is a direct extension of the game world. Use it with the same level of immersion as your in-game microphone. To post here, your character **must** physically possess a functional radio and a battery in-game.

* **Allowed:** Brief IC check-ins, organizing trade meets, distress calls, and spreading localized rumors.
* **Forbidden:** Hostile initiations (these must happen in-game), OOC flaming, or "keyboard warrior" toxicity.
* **Identity & Codenames:** You may use one (1) consistent codename, but it must be a name your character is actually known by in-game. Using an unknown alias to leak information you shouldn't have is considered powergaming.

### 🎗️ 9.5 Event Characters & NPC Encounters
Staff and Event Team members occasionally play unique characters marked by **Event Armbands**. 
* **Rules of Engagement:** You *are* allowed to KOS these characters, but doing so is a massive waste of story. These characters carry "Self-Destruct" gear that is ruined upon death—meaning you get zero loot and lose 100% of the roleplay.
* **The Golden Rule:** You are **never** permitted to remove the Event Armband from a living NPC character. Doing so will result in an immediate **3-day suspension** without the possibility of appeal.
